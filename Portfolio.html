Project: AIR Guatemala
Brand Redesign

Project Overview

We noticed AIR’s donation page was inconsistent, and created a disparity on whether users could trust the site. In addition to that, there was too much information on each page, discouraging users from reading anything. Overall, the website had a visually unpleasant style.

We created a style guide to give users a more visually appealing and consistent experience. We also cut down on information and gave users a “read more” option in certain areas.
